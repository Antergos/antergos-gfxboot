Inštalácia - bez ACPI

Current hardware usually requires ACPI (Advanced Configuration and Power Interface) to control the interrupt handling. ACPI completely replaces the old APM system.

Select Inštalácia - bez ACPI if you encounter problems during boot of the kernel. Known problems with machines that have problems with ACPI are:

  • kernel freezes when booting
  • PCI Cards are not detected or initialized properly

You may also try the Test firmvéru boot option and look at the test results of the ACPI validation.
